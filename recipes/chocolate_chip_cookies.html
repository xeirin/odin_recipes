Chocolate Chip Cookies

Moist and Chewy

A classic treat that you can eat whenver you crave something sweet. You can never go wrong with chocolate chip cookies.

This recipe makes approx. 24 cookies.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter
  • 1 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups flour
  • 3/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips/chunks

Steps

Note:Preheat your oven to 175°C (350°F) for 10 minutes before you put the cookie dough in.

  1. Melt the butter in a pan over medium heat for around 5-8 minutes until it turns golden brown, then pour into a bowl.
  2. Add in the brown and white sugar, then mix.
  3. Add the egg, egg yolks, and vanilla extract, then whisk until it reaches a caramel-like consistency.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix in the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Incorporate the flour mixture to the liquid mixture using a spatula.
  6. Mix in the chocolate chunks to the dough, then let the dough rest for about 15 minutes.
  7. Scoop or roll the dough into balls and place them on the baking tray lined with parchment paper. Make sure to leave spaces between the cookies so they could spread properly.
  8. Bake for around 10 to 15 minutes
  9. Take the cookies but leave them on the tray for 10 minutes or until they become firm since its still being baked on the hot tray.
  10. Transfer the cookies to a cooling rack and then its ready to eat. Enjoy!
